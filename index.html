Collage Widget

Turn your photos into hand-torn paper cutouts and arrange them as a collage on your Android home screen.

Everything happens on your device. Photos are never uploaded — the app doesn't even request internet access.

How it works

  • Pick photos from your gallery.
  • The subject is cut out on-device, with no server involved.
  • Each cutout gets an irregular, hand-torn paper edge.
  • Arrange the pieces — drag, pinch, rotate, resize, layer.
  • Add the finished collage to your home screen as a widget.

Also

  • Backgrounds: transparent, your own photo, gradients, or generated paper, cork, wood and linen textures.
  • A live clock overlay, ticked by the system so it costs no battery.
  • Prebuilt templates to start from.
  • Resizable widgets — as many as you like, each with its own collage.
